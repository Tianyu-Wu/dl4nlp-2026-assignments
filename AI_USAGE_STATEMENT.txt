AI Usage Statement
==================

AI assistance was used across the assignments in this repository
as a learning and debugging aid. Specifically, AI was used to:

- Explain concepts and clarify assignment requirements
- Review and give feedback on code written by the student
- Help interpret error messages and debug issues

All code implementations were written by the student. AI was not used to generate
complete solutions; its role was to support understanding of the material rather
than to produce answers directly.
