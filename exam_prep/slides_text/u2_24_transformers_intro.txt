the Transformer: main idea

· the Transformer uses attention for information
flow: “Attention is all you need”
· it was developed to mitigate drawbacks of RNNs
· it was originally designed for machine translation,
and the original design included two modules:

· an encoder that “summarizes” an input sentence
· a decoder that generates an output
