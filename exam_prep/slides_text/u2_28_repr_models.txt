BERT (Devlin et al., 2019)

· BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from
Transformers) is an architecture for transfer
learning
· Pushed the state of the art for several tasks
· Uses the encoder part of the Transformer
(originally 12 or 24 layers)
· Key technical point: attention is bidirectional

Takeaways

A representation model maps a document or a word to a
numerical representation

Prototypical representative: BERT

A relevant pre-training task is required

Originally, main focus was transfer learning: pre-training and
specialization

More recently, the main use case is in document retrieval and
other similarity-based tasks
