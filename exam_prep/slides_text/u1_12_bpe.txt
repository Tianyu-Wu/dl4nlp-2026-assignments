Byte Pair Encoding

Byte Pair Encoding (BPE) is an algorithm for learning subword 
tokens from text. 

έΑλ Step ΎβΕ:  Encode the text into a sequence of bytes. Initialise the 

token vocabulary with all single bytes. 

έΑλ Step ΎβΖ:  Create a new token by merging the most frequent pair of 

consecutive tokens. Add the new token to the vocabulary. 

έΑλ Repeat the previous step as long as the token vocabulary does not 

exceed a predeΎυΒned maximum size.

Sennrich et al. (2016)
