Learned position embeddings

GPT-1/2/3 (Radford et al., 2018) and
BERT (Devlin et al., 2019) used learned
position embeddings

One embedding for every unique position
(up to max length)

Fixed or learned: pros and cons

Fixed sinusoidal: nothing to learn, model
may (theoretically) learn to extrapolate to
longer sequences

Learned: easy to code, works well in
practice, but does not extrapolate

Takeaways and further notes

Transformers differ in their representation of positions

As of 2025, RoPE is the dominant method: used by Llama, Qwen,
Olmo, gpt-oss, DeepSeek, …

Later, we will discuss how to process very long sequences

Side note: all methods described here have 2D counterparts
